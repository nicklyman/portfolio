Two Reasons I Want to Learn to Code

The photos below show some of the places that I have been fortunate to travel to, spend time at, and I hope to be able to visit more parts of our world. The career of software developers offers more locational flexibility than either of my previous careers in GIS and remote sensing. That reason alone is one part of my decision to change careers, the other is that I am genuinely interested in software and it's potential to help make positive changes to our planet.
